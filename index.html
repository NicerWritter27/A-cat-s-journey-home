Chapter 1
The start of the long journey

A cat owner was going to an airport, far away from his home to meet his family with his cat.
When they arrived at the airport, the owner left the cat on the car, with the window left open so the cat wouldn't suffocate.
As the owner entered the ariport, the cat saw some food outside, with a stray cat that looked exactly like him.
So the cat got out of the car and chased away the stray cat, then the owner's cat started eating the food.
The stray cat jumped into the car, after that, the owner and his family entered the car and drove off, thinking that the stray cat is their cat.


Chapter 2
A new life

As the owner's cat finished eating, it starts to rain. The owner's cat then goes to where his owner used to park.
But when he arrives, the owner's car is not there anymore, the cat then looks for his owner in the parking lot.
And after realizing his owner's not anywhere, the cat then tries to look for him outside the parking lot.
