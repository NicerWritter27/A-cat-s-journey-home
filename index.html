Chapter 1
The start of the long journey

A cat owner was going to an airport, far away from his home to meet his family with his cat.
When they arrived at the airport, the owner left the cat on the car, with the window left open so the cat wouldn't suffocate.
As the owner entered the ariport, the cat saw some food outside, with a stray cat that looked exactly like him.
So the cat got out of the car and chased away the stray cat, then the owner's cat started eating the food.
The stray cat jumped into the car, after that, the owner and his family entered the car and drove off, thinking that the stray cat is their cat.


Chapter 2
A new adventure

As the owner's cat finished eating, it starts to rain. The owner's cat then goes to where his owner used to park.
But when he arrives, the owner's car is not there anymore, the cat then looks for his owner in the parking lot.
And after realizing his owner's not anywhere, the cat then tries to look for him outside the parking lot.
Fortunately, the rain is only light, so the cat can walk through the streets of the city, while avoiding the people's feet.
The cat then spots a recently thrown hotdog in the floor, and the cat eats it. As he is eating, another stray cat appeared and chased the cat.
As he was scared off, he ran to an alley. The cat then enters the carbage bin and started looking for food.
While he was eating, a person came out of a back door, while holding trash, as he approached the bin, the cat got scared and attacked him.
The cat then rans away as the person called for the animal control. And on the bright side, the rain stopped.


Chapter 3
On the run

As the rain stops, the cat dries himself. But with the animal control called, his way back home became longer.
As the animal control has reached the alley, they questioned the attacked person, then started tracking the cat.
The cat, unaware of the new threat, walks on the streets in hopes of finding his owner again.
The cat then unknowingly walks into a hotel, after a while, a staff eventualy finds him and scares him off.
As the cat runs away, the staff calls for the animal control for help. The animal control rushed immediately.
The staff told the animal control everything that had happend, and the animal control got closer to tracking the cat.
The cat spots a hotdog stand on the street with hotdogs being fried and customers waiting in line.
The cat approached a customer and started meowing, the customer tries to ignore him but then feels guilty and buys him a hotdog.
As the cat eats, the animal control that was tracking the cat down found him and started sneaking towards him.
The animal control then tries to catch the cat but misses slightly and the cat manages to escape and run away.
The animal control chases him. They reached an alley and the cat went through a hole on the fence and escaped the animal control.
As the animal control lost the cat, they started tracking the cat again, and the cat may not be so lucky next time.
The cat climbed up the garbage bin and to the top of the boxes that was on top of the garbage bin and climbed the emergency stairway.
The cat kept climbing and found an open window, he enters it and the apartment's owner wasn't there
